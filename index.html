      MEGA.nz Login

      Important Information

      • This login screen is not an official MEGA application; it only uses the MEGA SDK to access your account.
      • Your login credentials (email, password) are not saved or stored by this application.
      • All operations are performed securely between your device and MEGA's servers.
      • If you have Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) enabled on your account, you cannot log in due to current SDK limitations.

      Storage Information

      • When not logged in, all your records are stored in your browser's local storage.
      • When you log into your MEGA account, the app only shows and saves records in your cloud account.
      • To access your local records again, simply log out of your MEGA account.
